## Original source
> Effective March 1, 2026, the U.S. Small Business Administration's updated Standard Operating Procedure 50 10 8 made a fundamental change to how SBA 7(a) small loans are evaluated. The SBA discontinued its automated Small Business Scoring Service, known as the SBSS score, for federally regulated lenders. In its place, lenders must now conduct full commercial credit analysis using the same standards applied to conventional commercial loans.
