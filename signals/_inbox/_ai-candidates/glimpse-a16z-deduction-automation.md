# Glimpse / AI-Automated Retail Deduction Recovery

> Recover revenue that is systematically withheld through opaque financial deductions before it is written off as a cost of doing business.

## Anti-hype marker
The problem is not dispute complexity but sheer fragmentation — the AI wins by eliminating the login-and-gather step, not by making better legal arguments.

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** CPG brands manually logged into multiple retailer portals, pulled scattered documents, and reconciled line items to dispute invalid deductions — a fragmented, labor-intensive process where many invalid deductions went uncontested and became permanent revenue leakage.
- **To:** AI agents log into retailer portals autonomously, centralize deduction documents, classify each as valid or invalid against internal supply chain and promotion records, and file disputes automatically — compressing a multi-week process to days and creating a compounding data advantage with each new integration.
