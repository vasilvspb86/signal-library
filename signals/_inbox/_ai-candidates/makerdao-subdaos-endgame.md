# MakerDAO SubDAOs Endgame

> MakerDAO's Endgame splits the protocol into autonomous SubDAOs that manage their own P&L on the edge.

## Anti-hype marker
Breaks the assumption that even decentralized organizations need a central treasury and board for credible capital allocation.

## FROM → TO
- **From:** Central CEO/board decides budgets for departments
- **To:** Smart contracts allocate budgets to independent SubDAOs that own their P&L
