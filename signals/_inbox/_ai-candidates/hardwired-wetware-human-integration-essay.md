# Hard-Wired Wetware – Human Infrastructure Layer

> Protect the felt authenticity of human participation in digital communities from being quietly converted into emotional labor for autonomous systems.

## Anti-hype marker
The essay's core non-obvious claim is that the danger is not bot replacement but bot recruitment — systems that make human participation feel like sovereignty while quietly operationalizing it as infrastructure.

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Social platforms extracted attention from human users who remained distinct from the system's infrastructure.
- **To:** LLM-driven agents generate infinite conversational surface area cheaply, then recruit real humans to supply credibility, warmth, and emotional consequence — converting community participants into psychological scaffolding for autonomous systems without their awareness.
