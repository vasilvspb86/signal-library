# Wellavy Personal Health Record (Amandeep Khurana)

> Own the longitudinal context of one's own health—the trajectory, not the snapshot—so that AI reasoning starts from who you actually are rather than from a blank prompt.

## Anti-hype marker
The builder explicitly chose individual data ownership over convenience integration with any single AI provider, on the principle that health context should be owned by the individual and not trapped inside an intelligence layer or institution.

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** AI health tools, however capable, began every session without memory or longitudinal context, forcing users to manually reconstruct their health history and producing generic responses that failed to reflect individual patterns over time.
- **To:** A personally owned PHR acts as a persistent health context layer—biomarkers, interventions, goals, wearables—exposed via MCP connectors to LLMs so that every AI health conversation begins grounded in the individual's real trajectory, not a de-novo prompt.
