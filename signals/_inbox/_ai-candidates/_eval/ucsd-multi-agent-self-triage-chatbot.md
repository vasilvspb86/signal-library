# UC San Diego Multi-Agent Self-Triage System

> Make sense of symptoms clear enough to decide whether to act, grounded in clinician-auditable reasoning rather than unverified information.

## Anti-hype marker

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Self-triage depended on internet searches that were overwhelming, impersonal, and medically unverified, leading to unnecessary ER visits or dangerous delay.
- **To:** A conversational AI grounded in AMA-validated clinical flowcharts guides patients through structured self-assessment with traceable, clinician-auditable reasoning at every step.
