# BUTLR (MIT spin-out)

> Protect the right to move through shared spaces without one's individual presence being converted into a personally identifiable record.

## Anti-hype marker
Opaque cover that blocks visible light entirely makes the third-generation sensor architecturally indistinguishable from a standard ceiling fixture — privacy compliance is physical, not just contractual

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Badge-based occupancy data that logs access events but cannot capture dwell time, activity type, or conference room utilisation — and actively obscures real utilisation gaps
- **To:** Thermal infrared sensing that generates spatial intelligence (location, headcount, dwell time, posture, activity state) without capturing any image or identifying individuals
