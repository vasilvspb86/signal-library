> The first is bias. An engine trained on yesterday's lending data tends to freeze yesterday's exclusions into tomorrow's decisions - exclusions that have repeatedly penalised first-time founders, women-led MSMEs, and borrowers outside the metros. The second is explainability. A machine's decision must remain defensible to a regulator, an auditor, and to the borrower whose loan it shapes.
