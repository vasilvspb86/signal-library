# Medly

> Reclaim the right to be fully heard in a clinical encounter by arriving with one's own symptoms already translated into the language clinicians use.

## Anti-hype marker
Positions the patient as the author of their own clinical record rather than a passive information source — a role inversion medicine has historically resisted.

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Patients arrived at appointments with unstructured symptom memories that clinicians had to decode in under seven minutes, causing critical patterns to go unmentioned.
- **To:** A patient-controlled app converts personal symptom logs into clinical-grade SOAP notes, handing the patient a structured case for their own body before the encounter begins.
