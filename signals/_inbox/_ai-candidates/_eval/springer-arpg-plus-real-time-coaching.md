# ARPG+ Real-Time LLM Prompting Coach

> Construct durable prompting skill as a transferable capability rather than simply receive better answers in isolated moments.

## Anti-hype marker
The paper explicitly names the 'autonomy paradox' — AI coaching that improves output quality while eroding the metacognitive activity that makes learning durable — and designs against it

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Static prompt templates and rule-based hints that optimize AI outputs without building the learner's own prompting competence
- **To:** A real-time coaching system that senses cognitive load and Zone of Proximal Development moment-to-moment, delivers calibrated interventions, and fades support as prompting skill genuinely develops
