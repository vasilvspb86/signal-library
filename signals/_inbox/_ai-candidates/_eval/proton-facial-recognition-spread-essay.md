# Proton Privacy — 'Smile, You're On Camera'

> Protect the irreversible biometric identity of the face from being captured, templated, and retained by private actors in every space people routinely pass through.

## Anti-hype marker
TSA's own figures suggest roughly 99% of passengers do not realise they can refuse facial scanning — making opt-in a legal fiction rather than a lived reality.

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Facial recognition was experienced as an opt-in identity convenience (phone unlock, airport fast-track) that individuals chose to use.
- **To:** Facial recognition is now the default processing layer in supermarkets, stadiums, theme parks, and transit — capturing all faces whether or not individuals are aware, with no federal legal floor in the US and patchy consent enforcement globally.
