# a21.ai Somatic Credit Framework

> Verify the living health of a business at the moment of lending, rather than through forensic documents from an outdated past.

## Anti-hype marker
Frames the credit score not as outdated but as a 'forensic artifact' — the intellectual claim that static underwriting is structurally incapable of perceiving a business that can become insolvent in 48 hours

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Underwriting built on tax returns 18 months out of date, quarterly balance sheets, and bureau scores that update slowly — a 'latency gap' that becomes systemic risk in high-volatility markets
- **To:** Agentic AI swarms ingest live bank feeds, ERP streams, and macro signals continuously, building a real-time 'digital twin' of business liquidity and issuing prescriptive credit adjustments with full reasoning trace

## Original source
> Somatic Credit is the practice of underwriting based on the "vital signs" of a business in real-time. It is the move from static snapshots to a continuous, agentic stream of financial health. By deploying autonomous AI agents that inhabit the transaction layer of an enterprise, lenders can now perceive risk with the same resolution that a doctor perceives a heartbeat. This is not merely "faster" underwriting; it is a fundamental re-engineering of the credit-trust relationship through Agentic AI.
