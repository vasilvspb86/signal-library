# Museum of Mosaic Environments — Presence Problem Design Research

> Preserve the felt experience of being fully inside a place — rather than documenting it from a safe, mediated distance — so that what is encountered actually registers and is carried home.

## Anti-hype marker
Cites psychedelic integration and BDSM aftercare literature as primary design sources — the work names the post-experience period as an ethical responsibility, not a UX nicety

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Immersive institutions designed for the experience as it happened inside the gallery, then dropped visitors at the door; post-visit memory consolidation, emotional integration, and re-entry into the city were undesigned gaps.
- **To:** A rigorous design research process drawing on psychedelic integration literature and BDSM aftercare frameworks establishes that the post-experience period must be intentionally designed — the Garden of Egress, the Afterglow bar, the Jinn AI — as a complete architecture of transition.
