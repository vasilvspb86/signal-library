# a21.ai Somatic Credit Framework

> Verify a business's financial health in real time rather than trusting a snapshot that may be a year out of date by the time a credit decision is made.

## Anti-hype marker
Frames continuous transaction monitoring as 'somatic' health—borrowing the medical vital-signs metaphor to distinguish it from faster-but-still-periodic fintech approaches

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Underwriting built on periodic, backward-looking documents—tax returns, balance sheets, bureau scores—that create a dangerous latency gap between reality and decision
- **To:** Autonomous AI agents inhabit a business's transaction layer continuously, building a live 'digital twin' of liquidity that enables credit decisions based on the current second

## Original source
> Somatic Credit is the practice of underwriting based on the "vital signs" of a business in real-time. It is the move from static snapshots to a continuous, agentic stream of financial health. By deploying autonomous AI agents that inhabit the transaction layer of an enterprise, lenders can now perceive risk with the same resolution that a doctor perceives a heartbeat.
