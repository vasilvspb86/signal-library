# Ralf Kubli — SME Credit Gap Structural Analysis

> Hold the global economy's smallest enterprises to the same standard of credit continuity that investment-grade borrowers have always enjoyed — the right to roll debt rather than prove solvency from scratch each cycle.

## Anti-hype marker
Identifies that payment innovation has been mistaken for finance innovation — 'shiny apps for foreign currency exchange are not innovation in finance' — and that the missing pieces are contract standardization and credit enhancement, not faster apps

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** SME lending innovations addressing only digital onboarding and underwriting models while leaving standardized financial contracts and credit enhancements unsolved — preventing securitization and trapping the credit gap at the same scale for decades
