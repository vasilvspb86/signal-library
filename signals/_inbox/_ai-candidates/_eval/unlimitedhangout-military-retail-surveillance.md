# Unlimited Hangout — 'The Military Retail Industrial Complex'

> Verify who actually controls biometric data collected in commercial spaces and whether that data can be accessed by state security agencies without the knowledge of the people it was taken from.

## Anti-hype marker
The retail-to-national-security data pipeline is not hypothetical: Flock Safety's license-plate data reaches ICE via local law enforcement pass-through, and Wegmans explicitly declined to say whether it shares biometric data with ICE.

## FROM -> TO
