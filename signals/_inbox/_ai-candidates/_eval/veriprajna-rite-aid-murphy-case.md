# Veriprajna / Rite Aid / Harvey Murphy — Deep AI Architecture Essay

> Protect against algorithmic systems that present probabilistic estimates as verified facts, stripping away the human's ability to challenge an automated accusation before severe consequences occur.

## Anti-hype marker
The closed-set / open-set mismatch — deploying models optimised to always find a match in environments where most people are not on any watchlist — is the structural root cause of mass false positives, and it is a known, preventable engineering choice.

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** AI accuracy claims were evaluated on benchmark datasets by the deploying organisation before rollout, providing at least a nominal assurance of performance in the real environment.
- **To:** Retailers deploy 'wrapper' facial recognition systems whose vendors disclaim all accuracy warranties; confidence scores are hidden from the humans acting on alerts; and a single algorithm output can result in arrest, imprisonment, and lasting harm before any human review occurs.
