# SymptomAI (Google / Fitbit)

> Establish an accurate diagnostic foundation before clinical consultation, transforming the encounter from exploratory to confirmation.

## Anti-hype marker
First real-world RCT of conversational health AI at meaningful consumer scale — using actual patients, not clinical vignettes — and the AI outperformed board-certified physicians.

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Consumer LLMs defaulted to user-guided symptom discussion, producing significantly lower diagnostic accuracy than a structured interview would.
- **To:** An agentic symptom-interview system deployed via Fitbit to nearly 14,000 real users outperforms independent clinicians in differential diagnosis accuracy by a factor of 2.47.
