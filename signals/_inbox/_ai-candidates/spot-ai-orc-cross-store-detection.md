# Spot AI — Cross-Store ORC Pattern Detection

> Connect incident data across an entire store network in real time so that organised criminal patterns become visible before the next location is targeted.

## Anti-hype marker
The competitive advantage is not the camera AI but the architectural shift from per-store incident response to portfolio-level pattern intelligence — a data aggregation insight rather than a computer vision breakthrough.

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Loss prevention data was siloed by store — video, POS exceptions, and incident reports lived in separate systems at each location, making cross-location patterns invisible until after a theft ring had moved on.
- **To:** Unified AI dashboards aggregate video, POS, and LPR data across 30–100+ stores into a single queryable platform, enabling cross-district ORC detection; All Star Elite achieved 83% reduction in cash shrink and cut investigation time by 50% after deployment.
