# Counsel Health × Oura Ring

> Receive clinical care grounded in what is actually happening in daily life rather than in the artificial snapshot of a clinic visit.

## Anti-hype marker
The integration explicitly uses Oura's continuous O2 and HRV data to differentiate clinical severity—e.g. respiratory virus vs. acute asthma—a diagnostic distinction previously impossible without a clinician present.

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Clinical assessment was constrained to what a patient could report and what instruments could measure during a brief, scheduled appointment in a facility.
- **To:** Continuous biometric data from a wearable informs real-time AI symptom assessment and seamless escalation to a licensed physician, all within a consumer app, collapsing the distance between daily health and clinical response.
