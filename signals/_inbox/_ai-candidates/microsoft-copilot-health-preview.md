# Microsoft Copilot Health

> Reclaim coherent understanding of one's own health by unifying fragmented data sources into a single, personally contextualised view before seeing a clinician.

## Anti-hype marker
Microsoft explicitly ring-fences health conversations from general Copilot and from training data, acknowledging that health data aggregation requires a distinct privacy contract not applicable to generic AI use.

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Health data existed across disconnected silos—wearables, lab results, appointment records—with no consumer-facing layer synthesising them into actionable personal guidance.
- **To:** A mass-market AI health layer (50M+ daily health queries across Microsoft) connects wearables, records from 50,000+ US providers, and conversational guidance in one secure space, positioning itself as the ambient health navigator before any clinical encounter.
