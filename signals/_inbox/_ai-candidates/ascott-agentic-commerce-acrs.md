# Ascott — Agentic Commerce Strategy

> Secure direct control over how AI agents discover and book your properties before platform intermediaries lock in the recommendation logic.

## Anti-hype marker
First major hospitality group to formally declare an agentic commerce strategy, treating the Accenture-Amadeus-EHL engagement as architecture, distribution, and people transformation simultaneously.

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Hotel inventory expressed as room-type plus rate-plan combinations, discoverable by human browsers navigating OTA interfaces.
- **To:** Attribute-based, machine-readable inventory via Amadeus CRS, with a first-party booking agent (Cubby) capable of transacting across external AI surfaces while preserving loyalty continuity.
