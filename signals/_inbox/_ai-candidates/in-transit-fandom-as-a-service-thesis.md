# Fandom as a Service Thesis (Martin Berg / In Transit)

> Recover recognition and economic return from the creative energy invested in a franchise, rather than donating that value to the IP holder for free.

## Anti-hype marker
Names a structural business model shift — not just a fan engagement tactic — with blockchain and generative AI as the infrastructure that makes value-splitting mechanically possible

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Fans created derivative content — fanfiction, UGC games, cosplay — that strengthened franchise IP and network effects without receiving any share of the value they generated
- **To:** Franchises can offer tooling, distribution, and monetization to fans as professional contributors, turning passionate audiences into co-creators and stakeholders who earn from the worlds they expand
