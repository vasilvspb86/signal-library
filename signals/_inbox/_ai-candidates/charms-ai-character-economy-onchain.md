# Charms.ai – Onchain Character Economy

> Establish durable ownership and economic stake in the fictional characters one cares about, rather than renting access from a platform that captures all the value.

## Anti-hype marker
The novel mechanism is the character treasury — a share of market fees flows to the character's own compute costs, creating a self-sustaining economic entity rather than a platform-dependent chatbot.

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Users paid subscriptions for access to AI characters they did not own; if a character became beloved, the value accrued entirely to the hosting platform.
- **To:** Charms gives every public AI character its own onchain economy with creator ownership, fan participation, and a character treasury funded by market activity — making beloved characters durable cultural assets with distributed upside.
