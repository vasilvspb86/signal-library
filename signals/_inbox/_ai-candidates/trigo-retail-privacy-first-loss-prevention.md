# Trigo Retail AI Loss Prevention

> Protect retail inventory at the moment of in-store concealment without storing or processing any biometric identity data from honest shoppers.

## Anti-hype marker
The privacy-by-design architecture is presented as a commercial differentiator rather than a regulatory concession, with the anonymised-figure approach explicitly marketed as enabling a frictionless experience for honest shoppers.

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Conventional surveillance focused on the checkout moment, leaving the majority of concealment events that occur mid-floor invisible until after the shopper exited.
- **To:** Computer vision tracks shoppers as anonymised figures, cross-references item pick-ups against checkout scans in real time, and alerts security while the shopper is still on site — with no biometric data collected or stored at any point.
