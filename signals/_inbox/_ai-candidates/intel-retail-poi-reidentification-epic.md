# Intel Retail Store-Wide Loss Prevention — POI Re-ID

> Protect stores from repeat known offenders by tracking their presence across camera feeds using body appearance — not facial recognition — to stay within legal boundaries while enabling real-time security response.

## Anti-hype marker
Intel is publishing the benchmark architecture openly — including its explicit 'face recognition is out of scope' constraint — as a transparency signal to the retail industry that compliant real-time re-identification is technically viable without biometrics.

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Identifying a known person of interest required facial recognition, carrying significant legal and ethical exposure, or relied entirely on human memory and static watchlist photos reviewed manually.
- **To:** Intel's open-source retail AI suite uses body-appearance ReID embeddings (explicitly excluding facial recognition) to enrol and re-identify persons of interest across up to 100 cameras in real time, with the legal and compliance decisions left entirely to the deploying partner.
