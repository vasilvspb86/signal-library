# Wilkhahn Office on Stage / In-Between Spaces

> German furniture maker Wilkhahn positions 'In-between spaces' as cognitive infrastructure for tacit knowledge exchange — not as chairs and desks.

## Anti-hype marker
Inverts the office furniture pitch from durability/cost-per-seat to 'time-to-prototype' and density of accidental collisions.

## FROM → TO
- **From:** Office furniture sold to facility managers as durable, modular hardware
- **To:** Office furniture sold to CEOs/HR as an instrument that grows tacit knowledge and serendipitous collisions
