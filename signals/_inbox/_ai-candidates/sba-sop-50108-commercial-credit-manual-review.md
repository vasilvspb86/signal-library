# SBA SOP 50 10 8 — End of Automated SBSS Credit Scoring

> Forcing small business borrowers and their advisors to actively manage multi-bureau commercial credit profiles that were previously invisible because a single automated score obscured them.

## Anti-hype marker
A regulatory rollback of automation — creating a new human-review burden precisely as the industry accelerates toward AI automation in lending

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** SBA 7(a) small loans were evaluated via the automated SBSS score, a single summarized number that let lenders make rapid go/no-go decisions without deep bureau analysis.
- **To:** Every SBA lender must now manually evaluate borrower profiles across Dun & Bradstreet, Equifax Business, and Experian Business with the rigor of conventional commercial loans, making profile completeness and accuracy directly decisive.
