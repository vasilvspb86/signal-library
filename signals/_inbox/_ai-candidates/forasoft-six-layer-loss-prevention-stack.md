# Fora Soft / Six-Layer Edge AI Loss Prevention

> Protect store revenue by making every transaction and movement machine-observable at the moment it happens, not after the damage is done.

## Anti-hype marker
Edge-first inference economics, not cloud AI, are the enabling constraint — unit economics break above 4 cameras on cloud compute, making the architectural choice determinative.

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Loss prevention relied on spot audits, human floor patrols, and post-hoc POS exception reports, catching perhaps 5% of shrink events and attributing variance to an opaque store-level bucket.
- **To:** A six-layer edge-AI stack — camera fleet, on-premises inference, custom detection models, POS event correlation, tiered operator triage, and POS integration — delivers real-time anomaly alerts across self-checkout, exit, receiving, employee, and ORC use cases with 4–9 month payback.
