# Hims & Hers Conversational Patient Intake

> Receive care that starts from what the patient actually says rather than from the constraints of a form designed for institutional convenience.

## Anti-hype marker
Every prescription is still reviewed and signed by a licensed clinician—the conversational intake is a structured input to clinical judgment, not a replacement for it, which is why it scaled without regulatory crisis.

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Telehealth intake used static multi-page questionnaires that flattened patient narrative into checkbox selections, losing the clinical signal most useful to a prescribing clinician.
- **To:** Hims & Hers built a $5B telehealth business on conversational, branching, free-text intake that captures patient narrative, adapts in real time to contraindication signals, and completes in under 9 minutes on mobile—now the de-facto bar for the sector.
