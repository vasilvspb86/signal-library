# Chief Outsiders Fractional Executives

> Senior leaders refuse single-employer loyalty and sell C-level expertise on a subscription/fractional basis.

## Anti-hype marker
Breaks the implicit norm that executive talent must be exclusive and full-time to be credible.

## FROM → TO
- **From:** Top managers are full-time employees of one company
- **To:** Top managers are autonomous nodes plugged into 3–4 companies simultaneously by the slice

## Curator notes
- Compas segment 3 (Autonomy) — the human task is about controlling one's own expertise as a resource, not connecting with others (4).
- Diffusion stage Early Adopter — Chief Outsiders has existed for years, but the broader fractional-CXO pattern is still scaling outside the US.
- Part of the 'Corporation of One' macro pattern called out by the author across multiple signals in this article.
