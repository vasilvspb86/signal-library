# NCR Voyix / Multi-Signal Hybrid Checkout System

> Protect transaction accuracy at checkout by making item identification continuous and multi-sourced rather than dependent on a single scan event a human may skip or misrepresent.

## Anti-hype marker
The shrink-detection value emerges as a byproduct of improving checkout accuracy — the system was not designed primarily as loss prevention but becomes one by fusing sensor streams.

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Checkout item identification was a linear, single-signal process — one barcode scan per item — creating a gap where missed scans were invisible to the system until inventory variance surfaced the loss.
- **To:** Multiple overhead cameras capture items from different angles as they move on the conveyor belt, cross-referenced with RFID sensor data and fed to an item-identification ML model; mismatches between computer-vision item count and RFID item count trigger attendant intervention as a potential shrink event.
