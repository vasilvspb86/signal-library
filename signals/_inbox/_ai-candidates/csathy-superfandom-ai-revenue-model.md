# Superfandom as AI Killer App (Peter Csathy essay)

> Participate directly in the creative world of a beloved franchise rather than watching studio-controlled content from a distance.

## Anti-hype marker
The Disney/OpenAI deal frames IP as a production input for fan creativity rather than a protected output — a reversal of decades of IP orthodoxy, enabled by supervised licensing rather than enforcement.

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** IP owners monetized fandom through one-way licensing — merchandise, sequels, theme parks — with fans as consumers, not co-creators.
- **To:** Licensed generative AI makes franchise IP a scalable, always-on participatory platform where superfans create, share, and pay micro-fees for the right to inhabit the world of their favorite characters.
