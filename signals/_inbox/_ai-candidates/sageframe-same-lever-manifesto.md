# The Same Lever — Andrew T. Marcus

> Give learners control over their own cognitive resources by exposing how every educational technology — including AI — is systematically bent toward institutional compliance and control rather than the cultivation of student agency.

## Anti-hype marker
AI has not broken the educational machine — it has revealed what the machine is: a compliance-sorting apparatus that has always suppressed the agency it now needs students to have

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Each new educational technology adopted by institutions to deliver content, measure compliance, and sort students more efficiently
- **To:** AI as the first tool that cannot be fully absorbed by the compliance architecture — revealing the system's suppression of agency and forcing the question of who education actually serves
