# Expedia Group / Split-Funnel Trust Gap Research

> Protect travelers' boundaries by ensuring their payment security, data privacy, and recourse rights are guaranteed before surrendering booking control to AI agents.

## Anti-hype marker
Trust barrier between AI discovery and AI transaction is structural, not temporal — agentic booking thesis requires fundamental rewrite

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Agentic AI assumed to take over end-to-end booking with travelers delegating full transaction authority to AI intermediaries, disrupting OTA and direct-channel dominance.
- **To:** Travelers use AI for discovery but refuse AI-completed transactions due to trust concerns about payment security, data handling, and absence of recourse — structurally decoupling discovery from conversion.
