# AI Vanguard / Multimodal Vision Returns Triage Framework

> Protect refund decisions from fraudulent image evidence by making visual assessment consistent, auditable, and calibrated to claim value before money moves.

## Anti-hype marker
The workflow insight is that vision output must be evidence, not verdict — the refund decision still requires policy, order data, and customer history, making the model a triage accelerator rather than an autonomous approver.

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Damage claim review was manual, inconsistent across reviewers, and took 5–15 minutes per claim — creating both a cost burden and an uneven standard for what constituted acceptable evidence of loss.
- **To:** Vision-capable language models assess customer-submitted photos for damage visibility, product match, and evidence completeness, routing high-confidence low-value claims to fast-track processing and flagging fraud signals (recycled images, EXIF anomalies) before human review.
