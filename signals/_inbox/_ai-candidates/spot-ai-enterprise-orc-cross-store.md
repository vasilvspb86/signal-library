# Spot AI — Enterprise Cross-Store ORC Detection

> Connect theft patterns across a portfolio of stores before the next location is targeted, rather than responding to each incident in isolation.

## Anti-hype marker
The primary value is not better detection per camera but the elimination of the data silo between stores — ORC's structural advantage has always been that it scales while detection stays local.

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** ORC detection happened store by store, leaving the cross-location patterns that define organised retail crime invisible until after multiple incidents had occurred.
- **To:** Unified video AI, licence plate recognition, and POS exception reporting across 30–100+ stores allow LP teams to query the full portfolio and identify temporal clustering, repeat vehicles, and co-ordinated behaviour in minutes rather than months.
