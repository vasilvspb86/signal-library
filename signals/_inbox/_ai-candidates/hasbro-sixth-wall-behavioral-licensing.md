# Hasbro Sixth Wall – Behavioral Licensing

> Protect the felt authenticity of a beloved character in interactive AI encounters so that fans are not betrayed by an off-brand or unsafe simulation.

## Anti-hype marker
The unit being licensed is personality rather than image — 'Behavioral Licensing' is an attempt to copyright interiority, which has no legal precedent and may be the franchise business model of the next decade.

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** IP owners could only license a character's visual appearance and name; personality and behavior in AI interactions were uncontrolled, producing unauthorized and often damaging chatbot versions.
- **To:** Hasbro's CharacterOS codifies how a character thinks, speaks, and acts into a licensable behavioral spec, giving partners an authorized, brand-safe AI personality rather than an unpredictable simulation.
