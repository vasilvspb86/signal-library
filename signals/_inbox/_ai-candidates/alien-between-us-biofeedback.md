# The Alien Between Us (Laura Adima)

> A Dutch Design Week 2025 sculpture reads two strangers' pulses and breathing in real time, surfacing emotional changes neither party wants to notice on their own.

## Anti-hype marker
Articulates the inverse of the self-knowledge trend — 'we don't want to be aware' — and outsources the awareness work to a shared sensor object.
