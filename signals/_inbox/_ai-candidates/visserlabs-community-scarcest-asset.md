# Jordi Visser – Community as Scarcest Asset

> Establish a felt sense of belonging anchored in shared conviction rather than shared geography or occupation, as AI dissolves the institutional structures that previously manufactured identity.

## Anti-hype marker
The essay's non-obvious claim is that technology moves at the speed of capital but community rebuilds at the speed of trust — the gap between those two speeds is where the crisis lives, not in the technology itself.

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Community and identity were manufactured by default institutions — employers, nation-states, professional guilds — whose shared infrastructure shaped who people were.
- **To:** As AI strips occupational identity and dissolves institutional belonging, intentional communities organized around shared conviction and mutual contribution become the scarce infrastructure that meaning and trust route through.
