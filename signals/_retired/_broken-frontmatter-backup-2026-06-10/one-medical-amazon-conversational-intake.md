# One Medical / Amazon Primary Care Onboarding

> Reclaim the time and dignity lost to administrative friction by making the front door to primary care feel like a conversation rather than a bureaucratic obstacle.

## Anti-hype marker
Amazon has deliberately not shipped a patient-facing generative-AI care navigator, keeping AI in scheduling and intake summarisation rather than diagnosis—a conservative boundary that defines the credible near-term model.
