# "The Death of the Secret" (The Structural Lens)

> Protect the unspoken interior of a person—the thoughts, dispositions, and health states never disclosed—from being reconstructed by inference engines that require no confession and breach no record.

## Anti-hype marker
The ETH Zurich study at the essay's centre demonstrates the mechanism on real Reddit profiles with no hacking, no breach, and no sensitive data collected—making it invisible to every existing regulatory category.

## FROM -> TO
