# "When the AI Did It, Who Did It?" — Legal Accountability Essay

> Establish that 'the AI decided' cannot function as a complete legal sentence — and that responsibility must flow upward to the humans who built, deployed, and profited from the system.

## Anti-hype marker
The essay frames facial recognition wrongful arrest and AI companion manipulation as the same underlying legal deficit — the absence of a 'law of machine delegation' — unifying what appear to be separate AI harms under a single structural diagnosis.
