# "Student Success in the Age of AI" (Steve Hargadon)

> Establish agency as the non-delegable core of learning at the exact moment AI makes every delegatable cognitive task free and instant.

## Anti-hype marker
Argues the two historically competing arguments for agency — intrinsic and instrumental — are now structurally identical: for the first time in mass education, what is true and what is useful have stopped diverging

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** School rewarded costly, effortful compliance — the credential was a meaningful signal because it was expensive to simulate
- **To:** AI makes compliance free to simulate, collapsing the credential's signal value; agency — setting direction, judging quality, owning results — becomes the only input machines cannot supply
