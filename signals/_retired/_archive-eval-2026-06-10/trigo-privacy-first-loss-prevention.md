# Trigo Retail Computer Vision Loss Prevention

> Protect inventory from concealment-based theft at the moment it happens — inside the store, before checkout — without collecting or storing any biometric identity data on shoppers.

## Anti-hype marker
The privacy-by-design architecture is not a regulatory concession but the core product differentiator — anonymisation is what makes the system deployable across EU and UK jurisdictions where biometric retail surveillance is legally precarious.

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Loss prevention AI focused on checkout anomalies, leaving in-aisle concealment — the primary method used by shoplifters — invisible to automated systems.
- **To:** Trigo's computer vision platform tracks shoppers as anonymised figures, cross-references items picked up against checkout scans in real time, and alerts security while the shopper is still on-site — without facial recognition or biometric templates, deployed on existing CCTV infrastructure.
