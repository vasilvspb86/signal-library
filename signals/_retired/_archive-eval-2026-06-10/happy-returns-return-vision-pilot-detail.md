# Happy Returns – Return Vision Pilot Mechanics (First-Party Account)

> Protect the physical intake moment from decoy substitution by coupling behavioural risk scoring with on-hub computer-vision verification before any refund is released.

## Anti-hype marker
Under 1% of returns are flagged, making the system precise enough to avoid degrading the experience for the 99%+ of honest shoppers.

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Returns verification stopped at the Return Bar front desk; hub-level inspection was manual and inconsistent.
- **To:** A two-layer system scores behavioural risk at initiation and then photographs flagged items at the hub, using AI to compare against catalogue images — averaging $218 in prevented loss per flagged return.
