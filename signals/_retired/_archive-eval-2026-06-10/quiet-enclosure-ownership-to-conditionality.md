# Matthias Muhlert — The Quiet Enclosure

> Reclaim durable rights over tools and spaces rather than accepting the incremental conversion of ownership into revocable, monitored, priced-per-use relationships.

## Anti-hype marker
Frames the surveillance-subscription shift not as convenience but as a transfer of labour, risk, and time downward to the individual — names what is lost rather than celebrating what is gained

## FROM -> TO
- **From:** Ownership conferring independence — a durable claim on things and services that the owner controls
- **To:** Access conferring permission — a revocable relationship in which terms change unilaterally, functionality can be degraded, and behaviour is continuously monitored as a condition of use
