# Gaetano Pesce: The Chiat\Day New York Project (Pulp Galerie, Paris)

> Reclaim the radical proposition that physical workspace should be designed around how people actually move and collaborate — not around fixed hierarchy and assigned territory.

## Anti-hype marker
A 1994 experiment now recognized as the first hot-desking proposal in history — the exhibition surfaces how far ahead of its moment the vision was, and how much of its playfulness was lost when the flex office became mainstream

- **To:** Fluid, mobile, paperless environment where spatial form refuses hierarchy and invites intuitive movement through shared space
