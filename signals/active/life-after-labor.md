# Life After Labor

> Crisis of purpose and the asceticism of mutual belonging in a post-automated world.

## Anti-hype marker
The phenomenon goes far beyond the populist discussions of Universal Basic Income (UBI) or AI automation of routine. The real shift is fixed in deep existential discomfort and the search for new forms of "curative asceticism", where community and belonging form not around shared earnings or corporate KPIs, but through sharing common deficits, conscious limitations, and collective unconscious practices (such as the Social Dreaming method).

## FROM → TO
- **From:** Self-realization and status validation through profession, career advancement, material comfort, and utilitarian security.
- **To:** Searching for meaning through conscious discomfort, voluntary commitments, participation in complex group dynamics, and meta-competence in managing one's own attention.
