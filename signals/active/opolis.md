# Opolis

> Nomadic trust ecosystems.

## Anti-hype marker
Not just another resource-sharing service for budget savings (which would only optimize an existing process). The real shift is a radical filter change: people pool critical life-support infrastructure (safety, taxes, insurance) not by geography or employer, but by social-capital proportionality and mutual values audit.

## FROM → TO
- **From:** Relying on external vertical institutions, insurance companies, and large corporations for personal safety and stability.
- **To:** Delegating personal safety, insurance, and life-support functions to an autonomous peer environment matched by values and social capital.
